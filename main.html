Common Cyber Threats in India

Stay aware of the most common online scams and learn how to protect yourself.

🐟

Phishing Emails

Fake emails pretending to be from banks, government, or popular services.

  • ✔️ Check sender email address
  • ✔️ Look for spelling errors
  • ✔️ Never click suspicious links
📞

Fake OTP Calls

Scammers call pretending to be bank officials asking for OTP.

  • ✔️ Banks never call for OTP
  • ✔️ Verify caller identity
  • ✔️ Hang up and call bank directly
