Common Cyber Threats in India

Understanding these threats is the first step toward protection. Learn to recognize the warning signs.

Phishing Emails

Fake emails pretending to be from banks, government, or popular services.

  • Check sender email address
  • Look for spelling errors
  • Never click suspicious links
üìû

Fake OTP Calls

Scammers call pretending to be bank officials asking for OTP.

  • Banks never call for OTP
  • Verify caller identity
  • Hang up and call bank directly

UPI Scams

Fake payment requests, QR code scams, and payment reversal tricks.

  • Verify before paying
  • Use trusted contacts only
  • Enable transaction alerts

Identity Theft

Stealing personal information to commit fraud or open accounts.

  • Share minimal personal info
  • Use strong passwords
  • Monitor financial statements
